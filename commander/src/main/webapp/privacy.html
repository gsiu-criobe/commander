TED Commander Privacy Statement

Introduction

Energy, Inc. manufactures and markets electricity monitoring equipment under the brand name “TED – The Energy Detective.” The TED-brand of products allow one to view and manage electricity usage in real time within a residence/facility, or from remote locations using any mobile device or computer. To provide this ability, we must collect certain information from you. Our privacy policy describes how we collect, protect, handle and maintain the information that you provide and that we obtain through our cloud-based service, TED Commander. In this privacy policy, we use the term "System" to refer to the combination of TED products and services we provide, including the Services, User Experience, TED Devices and the Energy, Inc. Cloud Server ("Cloud Server") that interconnects, via the internet, the User Experience and TED Devices.

We consider your privacy to be very important. In this document, we describe the various kinds of information we collect, including your personally identifiable information, and how we treat it below. If you have any questions as to how the privacy policy applies to you, please email us at support@theenergydetective.com.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By installing and using any portion of the System, you agree to the provisions of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information we collect

We use the term "Information" to refer to any personally identifiable or anonymous information relating to you or your use of any portion of our System. We collect various categories of Information in connection with your use of the System and some of this information may specifically identify you. The following sections describe more specific categories of Information:

1.1 Account Information

Account Information is information about your account, including your Contact Information and Service Information (both defined below) and other account related information that is necessary or useful for us to manage your account, your subscription to optional features or services offered through the Services and privacy preferences.

Account Information does not include any personally identifiable Sensing Information. Account Information may be provided by you or a technology provider, service provider, third party website, merchant involved in the development and/or provision of the functionality of the System, and other third parties with whom we work.

Contact Information means information that allows someone to identify or contact you, including, for example: your name, address, telephone number, mobile telephone number, email address, and payment information if applicable.

Service Information means information necessary or useful to us in providing the System to you. Examples of Service Information include your ZIP Code, home system configuration (including appliances you set up to be monitored by the System, schedule, state, mode, rules; utility rates, tariff structure and settings), TED Devices in your home (including types, model numbers and software version numbers). Certain details of the Service Information is collected and transmitted to us whenever a TED Device is connected to the Cloud Server, and by connecting your TED Devices to the Service you are agreeing to have this information transmitted to us. Such information is treated as described below.

1.2. Sensing Information

Sensing Information is information captured by TED Devices connected with the System, including the status and diagnostics information from your TED Devices, as well as the information collected by TED Devices, such as power and energy information, and your interaction with TED Devices. We receive this type of information from your TED Devices as they connect with the System.

1.3. Location Information

We may collect your exact location or relative location if you choose to enable your TED Devices to send us location information. We do not make Location Information accessible outside your account to anyone you did not explicitly share that information with. Location Information can be used, for example, to provide weather details pertinent to your location and to the System.

1.4. System Use Data

We automatically collect information regarding the actions you take on the System. For example, each time you use the System we automatically collect the type of Web browser you use, your operating system, your Internet Service Provider, your IP address, the pages you view, and the time and duration of your visits to the System. We use this information to help us understand how people use the System, and to enhance the services we offer.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like many websites, our website uses "cookies" to recognize you and to help personalize your online experience. We also use cookies to help us understand which parts of the websites are the most popular, where our users are going and how much time they spend at various locations within the websites. The information we collect through cookies is anonymous and we do not access any of your personally identifiable information through the cookies. A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server. These cookies contain information that can later be read by a web page server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. We use cookies to store information on your computer in order to save you time by eliminating the need to enter repeatedly the same information in order to display personalized content on later visits to the Websites.

You have the ability to decline cookies through your web browser. If you choose to decline cookies, however, you may not be able to use the features of the websites that depend on cookies.

The website may contain tiny electronic images known as web beacons. We may use web beacons to assist us in delivering cookies on our Website and to allow us to count users who have visited those pages. We may include web beacons in promotional email messages in order to determine whether messages have been opened and acted upon.

We use Google Analytics on the System to collect System Use Data, and to analyze how users use the System. For more information about how to opt out of having your information used by Google Analytics, visit https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/.
